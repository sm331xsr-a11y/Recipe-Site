Pizza

Back to Home | Back to Italian Cuisine

Preparation Time

30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese and pepperoni.
  5. Bake until the crust is golden and the cheese melts.
