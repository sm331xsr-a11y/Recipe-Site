Pasta

Back to Home | Back to Italian Cuisine

Preparation Time

25 minutes

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta until tender.
  2. Heat olive oil and garlic in a pan.
  3. Add tomato sauce and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Mix the pasta with the sauce.
  5. Serve with parmesan cheese.
