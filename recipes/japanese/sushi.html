Sushi

Back to Home | Back to Japanese Cuisine

Preparation Time

40 minutes

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori sheets
  • Salmon
  • Cucumber
  • Soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Cook the sushi rice and let it cool.
  2. Place a nori sheet on a bamboo mat.
  3. Spread rice over the nori.
  4. Add salmon and cucumber.
  5. Roll tightly and slice into pieces.
