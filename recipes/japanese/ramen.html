Ramen

Back to Home | Back to Japanese Cuisine

Preparation Time

35 minutes

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Chicken broth
  • Boiled egg
  • Green onions
  • Mushrooms

Instructions

  1. Heat the chicken broth in a pot.
  2. Cook the ramen noodles separately.
  3. Add the noodles to the broth.
  4. Top with boiled egg, green onions, and mushrooms.
  5. Serve hot.
